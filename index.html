Renato Velozo Ruiz

Welcome to my webpage! I am a Research Associate in the Deparment of Mathematics at Imperial College London. My mentor is Martin Taylor.

My research currently focuses on asymptotic stability results for kinetic equations. I am particularly interested in kinetic theory in general relativity.

Previously, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Toronto, and at the Sorbonne Université. I obtained my PhD in 2023 from the University of Cambridge under the supervision of Mihalis Dafermos and Clément Mouhot.

You can reach me via email at r.velozoruiz@imperial.ac.uk.
